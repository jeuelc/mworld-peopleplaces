The European colonisers had found the indigenous population didn't make good slave labour, especially when exposed to the hardships of plantation life.

The Atlantic slave trade which saw slaves captured in west Africa and transported by slave traders to the new world - became the key source for labour on sugar cane and coffee plantations throughout Latin America.

The slave trade changed the face of Latin America introducing new people with different languages and traditions and transforming the music, food and culture of the continent.
