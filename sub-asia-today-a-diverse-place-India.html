Did you know India is the planet's largest democracy? It has more than 1.2 billion people. It is also a country that is changing rapidly. Its economy is the 11th largest in the world, and over 35% of all professional positions are filled by women – three decades ago, this figure was just 4%.

India has had an undeniable influence on the rest of Asia. Much of this has had to do with historical patterns of emigration dating back as early as the 5th century CE (common era). Merchants and soldiers, priests and monks all helped spread Hinduism and Buddhism across the region. You can see the legacies of this today. For example, Bali is a Hindu island amid a Muslim nation, and in China, a localised form of Buddhism has evolved over the centuries. Today, elements of Indian culture have spread all around the world – from Bollywood movies and yoga to tandoori chicken and masala.
