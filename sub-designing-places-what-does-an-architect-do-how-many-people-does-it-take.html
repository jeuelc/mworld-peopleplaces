How Many People Does It Take?

Designing and making places is complicated! It takes a lot of hard work and involves many people all working together.

Landscape architects and urban designers help us think about what the spaces between the buildings will look like. Surveyors map out the land where we are going to build. Engineers work with architects to determine that the building and its foundations are sufficiently strong. Town planners help with the rules and regulations. Interior architects think about the inside of buildings. Sometimes an architect will work with an artist to incorporate an artwork into the building. Can you think of any spaces or places near you that have a sculpture or mural as part of the design?

Lighting designers make sure that the spaces look fantastic at night and are safe and visible. Builders construct the places that architects imagine - they work with people like carpenters, electricians and plumbers.

Communicating with all of these different types of people is one of the most interesting things about being an architect. You get to learn a little bit about lots and lots of interesting professions.
