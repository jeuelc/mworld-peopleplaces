From J.Lo to Shakira, Robert Rodriguez to Gael Garcia Bernal, Giselle Bundchen and Lionel Messi, Latin American icons in music, cinema, fashion and sport, are dominating global popular culture.

And that's not all!

In politics and economics, nations that were once described as part of the 'third world' or even 'underdeveloped' are now forging the way. Brazil, for example leads a controversial battle to have more diversified and democratic control of global standards for the internet.
