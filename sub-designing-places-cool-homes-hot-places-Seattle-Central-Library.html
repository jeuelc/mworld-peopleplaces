Have you been to the library recently? What do you think of first when you think of a library books right? But libraries are changing.

This amazing library in Seattle by OMA has turned how we think about designing and using libraries on its head. It still has books lots and lots of them - but it has many more things as well.

The Seattle Central Library is vibrant and colourful on the inside and from the outside it looks like it is doing the twist! This is because the architects have rotated the spaces inside to look at the different views of Seattle you can see from the building.

This library is a living room for the whole city of Seattle!
