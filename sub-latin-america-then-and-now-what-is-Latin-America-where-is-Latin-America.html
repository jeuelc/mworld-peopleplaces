Where is Latin America?

 
click here click here click here

You can find Latin America sandwiched between the Atlantic Ocean and the Pacific Ocean. But because its made up of the Spanish and Portuguese-speaking parts of the Americas (and sometimes the French parts) it's not exactly all of South America or all of North America.

Roughly speaking, Latin America stretches from the tip of South America to the north of Mexico, including some islands in the Caribbean Sea. But borders are funny things. Today there are so many Spanish-speakers in the southern United States and in New York (called 'Latinos') that we could say that Latin America also includes parts of the US.
