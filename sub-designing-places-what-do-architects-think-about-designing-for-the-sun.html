Designing For The Sun

Do you think about the weather much? Architects do too!

In places that have four seasons, it's important to keep the spaces we design cool in the summer and warm in the winter. There are many things that can help us do this but the easiest way is to look at the sun! In summer the sun is high in the sky and in winter it is low.

If we think about this in the right way we can save energy by not having to use heaters or air conditioners as much.

There are also places where it is cold or hot all year. In hot climates we can create shady spaces filled with plants, to cool us down with breezes. When it's cold we can face the windows toward the sun. If you live in a cold place isn't it nice to sit inside with the sun shining through the window, reading a good book, curled up like a cat?
