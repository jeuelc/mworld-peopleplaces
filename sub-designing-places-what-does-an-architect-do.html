What Does an Architect Do?

Have you ever wondered what an architect does?

Designing a really great place is complicated and involves many people. Architects work together with a team of people and their job involves many different things.

Architects listen to what people want and need in the spaces they design. They observe and analyse the site where the project will be located. They research, experiment and imagine. They sketch, draw and make models. They coordinate, collaborate and create.
