Where Do You Live?

To design a great place an architect needs to understand where the project is located. Does the land slope? Does it have lots of trees or a beautiful view? There might even be an important event, or some incredible history that occurred on the building site should this be included in the design?

The site is more than just land to build on. Architects need to understand how the things around the site might influence the design. Is it in the country or the city? Is the traffic loud? Are the buildings tightly packed? Is the street busy and bustling or quiet? Sometimes architects want to make the place they design fit in and sometimes they want to make it stand out.

While it is important to understand the site for practical reasons architects also draw inspiration from the place that they will build. If there is a beautiful view they might turn the building's windows towards it. If there are lots of trees they might imagine what it would be like to live high in the trees and raise the building up so you feel like you are living in a tree house!
