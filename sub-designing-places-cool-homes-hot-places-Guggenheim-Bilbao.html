Imagine having to clean the windows on this building!

What do you think is inside this building? Before it was built most people could not imagine that a building could look like this. It was designed by Frank Gehry with the help of a three dimensional computer program used by the aerospace industry - they make aeroplanes and space craft! This building is clad in thin sheets of titanium that curve and flow to create the spaces that enclose the art gallery inside.

Many people feel that it draws on the city for inspiration. It reminds some people of a ship on the river (a very crazy ship) perhaps Gehry was inspired by the ships in Bilbao's large commercial port. And other people think that the silhouette of the building looks like the hills surrounding the city. What do you think it looks like?
