A Big Decision

Why do people migrate to other countries?

In 2010, there were more than 214 million people living outside the country they were born in. This was about 3% of the world's population.There are lots of reasons why people decide to move to another country. Some move to find work and are lured by the prospect of earning more money than they would back home. Others want to flee war, conflict or unrest. Some people hope to be reunited with family members who have already made the move.

The reality for many migrants is that they move for a combination of reasons. Regardless of why they do it, the decision to migrate to another country is a big one to make. You leave behind family, friends and all thats familiar. The experience of migration has a huge impact on the life of the immigrant and the society that they join.
