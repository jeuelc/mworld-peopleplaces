What Do Architects Think About?

What kinds of things do architects think about when they design a building? What are the important considerations? Lets look at some of the key issues that architects think about.

Architects think about the people who will use the spaces they design and about the site where the project will be located.

They also think about how a building can use less energy. It takes a lot of energy to make a building and once a building is finished it may take a lot of energy to run it. If architects think cleverly about how the sun enters a building it not only makes us feel good but it helps to reduce our heating and cooling bills.

Architects also think about how a building fits together and what it is made of. And all good architects think about creating places that people want to live in. Architects also need to think about the future what type of house, town, city or even world will people want to live in then?
