Some houses do not look like they belong in the places they are part of. But this doesn't mean that the architect hasn't thought about how the building sits in its surroundings. Houses like this stand out from their surroundings and catch your eye.

The Truss Wall House by Ushida Findlay Architects looks completely different from any of the houses that are nearby. The architects wanted to create a feeling of joy in their buildings. And this building certainly looks like fun!

Here is a house that is made to look like a mythical creature called a gryphon. In fact it is called the Gryphon House. It was designed by architects Simon and Freda Thornton and it is actually an extension to an existing house. Have a look at it in the image cards - do you think it looks like the Gryphon is eating the old part of the house?
