Good architecture has a relationship to its location. There are different ways we can think about site and location. These houses are fantastic examples of work by architects who were inspired by their surroundings.

The first is by a very famous architect called Frank Lloyd Wright. It is called Fallingwater can you see why?

While Fallingwater feels as if it is a part of the rocks it is built over, the Rainforest house by Richard Leplastrier is raised on stilts. Have a closer look at the rainforest house can you imagine living high up in the canopy?
