In Mexico one of the most traditional and popular dishes is called 'mole' and it's made with chocolate. But its not the sort of sweet chocolate sauce that you might see poured over the top of a delicious bowl of ice-cream. Some of its other ingredients include chili peppers, onions and garlic and it's served with different types of meat and poultry!

So, there's a lot more to Latin American food than tacos, burritos, fajitas and tortillas! Sharing food with family and friends is a very important part of the cultures and peoples of Latin America.
