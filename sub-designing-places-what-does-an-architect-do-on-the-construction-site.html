When the working drawings are finished there is no time for napping! Next the project moves to the construction site. The architect often works with the builder to answer any questions they might have about the design and the drawings. Sometimes things don't go together in the way the architect imagined and the builder and the architect collaborate to find a solution.?

As you can imagine, a drawing is not a building. It is important to communicate and collaborate to get the project right. Buildings are complicated things. One of the best parts about designing a building or a place is seeing what you have imagined in your head become real!
