Your Future Home?

What do you think your home will be like when you grow-up? Where will you live?

The architecture of the future could look radically different from the way it does now. New technologies and new materials might mean we are able to build all kinds of crazy looking buildings and places. We might connect with our houses differently touch screens might take up whole walls or perhaps we will be sending people to live on Mars or the Moon! What do you think it might be like to live in these places?

The architecture of the future might look very similar to today's architecture. New technologies might not greatly change the 'look' of architecture and some of the things we like about the spaces we inhabit have not changed in a very long time.

Architects need to make places for all of the things that we love about our cities and homes.
