Do you live in an apartment building? Is it very, very tall? Where do you go outside to play? Do you play in the city or in a park? More and more people live in apartment buildings and units, especially in cities. We call this 'high density living'. Architects are interested in how people live in apartment buildings and how we might make them better places to live.

Architects can learn a lot from the way people already live and how they have lived in the past. The Tulou collective housing project in Guangdong Nanhai, China by URBANUS Architects is based on a traditional Hakka house or Tulou.
