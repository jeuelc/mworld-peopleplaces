A Global Experience

What are the advantages of having new immigrants to a country?

Although the United States is the largest recipient of immigrants in the world, it isnt the society in which immigration is an important issue. Many countries around the world accept immigrants. Cheap airfares mean it is affordable to move to another country, although there are many other reasons that determine whether immigration can occur. With the globalisation of the economy, people may need to move abroad for work whether they be temporary, long-term or permanent migrants.

The movement of people is likely to continue well into the future. Lets look at the experiences of other countries.
