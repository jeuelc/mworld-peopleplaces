This is Federation Square in Melbourne, Australia. It is a collection of public buildings arranged around a gently sloping public square. The triangular pattern on the skin of the building is inspired by fractal geometry and is made of sandstone, glass and zinc. There is a lot to do at Federation Square. It has an art gallery, a film centre, a visitor centre, offices, restaurants and bars. It has two main public spaces the outdoor Square and the enclosed Atrium and a beautiful lecture theatre with a great view of the Yarra River, which runs through the city.

Until Federation Square was built, Melbourne didn't have many great public outdoor spaces. Now, Federation Square is one of Melbourne's most important public places.

Not everyone likes the look of it, but most people agree that when you are there it is a brilliant place to be. Something is always happening at Federation Square.

Fractal Geometry

A fractal is a shape that is rough, or broken up into smaller parts. In art, these shapes are often made by computers, not by hand.
