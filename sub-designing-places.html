Designing Places

Architects think about buildings, but architecture is not just about buildings. To think about a building you also need to think about the spaces between the buildings so architecture is about more than just making buildings - it is about making places.

Architects think about the places we live in they imagine and rethink the spaces we use every day and they help us think about how we might use every day spaces in better and more interesting ways.

A good design for a building and its surroundings responds to the location it is built in and the people it is built for and makes places that people want to be in. Have you ever wondered what it takes to design a really fantastic place?
